INTRODUCING PRESTIGE FINSBURY PARK

Prestige Finsbury Park by Prestige developers is one such project that complements the fact that Bangalore has now turned out to be a fascinating real estate hub for profitable long-term investments. Prestige Finsbury Park is a 25 acre township destination, situated adjacent to the aerotropolis, within the IT / Aerospace & Hardware Park being developed by the KIADB. The park reserves a limited allotment for residential habitation, within which resides Prestige Finsbury Park.


Finsbury Park comprises of two developments; Hyde, spanned over 15 acres & Regent, covering 10 acres of in-demand land. Adjacently located, these two developments are independent of each other, holding their own set of identities, approvals, amenities and facilities.


Prestige Finsbury park- Hyde; a glorious high rise community, comprises of artistically designed one & two bedroom homes. Standing on 12 towers and climbing up to 22 floors, this colossal development consists of 2092 compactly designed, yet comfortable and naturally well-lit and ventilated apartments. Owing its gratitude to the expansive open spaces that surround each tower, these homes offer panoramic views that soothe the mind and spirit. To ensure that every need of the neighbourhood is met and to provide you and your family with the best recreational options, the community offers the luxury of a fully equipped clubhouse with a plethora of amenities, including a Swimming Pool, Gym, Health Club, Indoor Badminton Courts, Billiards, Table Tennis, Board Games, Reading Room, Party Halls, and a Crèche. To add on to that, there are provisions for a convenience store, salon, pharmacy & doctor’s clinic, mini theatre, space for ATM and much more.


The project is located at Bagalur Rd, Near St. Francis School, Gummanahalli in Bangalore. The location enjoys seamless connectivity to the rest of the city with its close proximity to the Bangalore International airport which further increases the land appreciation rates. ABOUT

Over the last decade, the Prestige Group has firmly established itself as one of the leading and most successful developers of real estate in India by imprinting its indelible mark across all asset classes. Founded in 1986, the group’s turnover is today in excess of Rs 3518 Cr (for FY 15); a leap that has been inspired by CMD Irfan Razack and marshaled by his brothers Rezwan Razack and Noaman Razack. Having completed 210 projects covering over 80 million sq ft, currently the company has 53 ongoing projects spanning 54 million sq ft and 35 upcoming projects aggregating to 48 million sq ft of world-class real estate space across asset classes. In October 2010, the Prestige Group also successfully entered the Capital Market with an Initial Public Offering of Rs 1200 cr. The Prestige Group today has become a name that is synonymous with innovation. The company has pioneered many landmark developments and introduced many firsts to South India.
